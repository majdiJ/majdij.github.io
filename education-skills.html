A-Level Computer Science NEA Project

For my A-Level Computer Science NEA (Non-Examined Assignment), I developed a School Register System using Visual Basic. This system allowed teachers to enroll students, save data in an SQL database, and track attendance efficiently. The project was designed with the needs of a lower income country in mind, and my client for this assignment was my cousin in Bangladesh - a teacher seeking a practical school management solution.

This hands-on project enabled me to enhance my skills in Visual Basic, object-oriented programming (via the use of classes), functional programming, agile methodologies, and the MoSCoW method. If youre intrested in my NEA project, have a look at the report:
NEA Project Document .

Extended Project Qualification (EPQ): Research on AI in Education

I also completed an EPQ - equivalent to half an A-Level - focusing on the societal impacts of Artificial Intelligence in education, with a special focus on generative AI. My research examined how AI can boost productivity for both students and teachers, while also addressing potential challenges such as over-reliance on technology and the loss of essential independent skills.

The project involved surveying peers, conducting in-depth research, and critically analysing the data, which in turn refined my skills in research, referencing, critical analysis, and presentation. For further insights and a detailed report on this EPQ project, please check out:
